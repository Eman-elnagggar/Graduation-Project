Quick Reference Guide

Ingredients to Avoid

  • Retinoids (Retinol, Tretinoin)
  • Salicylic Acid (high dose)
  • Parabens (in high concentrations)
  • Formaldehyde & Toluene
  • Hydroquinone
  • Phthalates

Use with Caution

  • Caffeine (limit 200mg/day)
  • Essential oils (concentrated)
  • Artificial sweeteners
  • Some fragrances
  • Sodium Lauryl Sulfate (SLS)
  • Chemical sunscreens

Generally Safe

  • Vitamin C (topical)
  • Hyaluronic Acid
  • Glycerin & Shea Butter
  • Mineral sunscreens (Zinc oxide)
  • Natural oils (Coconut, Jojoba)
  • Cocoa Butter
