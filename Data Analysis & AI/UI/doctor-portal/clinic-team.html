Assistant Permissions

Assistants CAN:

  • Manage appointment requests
  • Set and modify your availability
  • View patient contact information
  • Reschedule or cancel appointments
  • View appointment schedules

Assistants CANNOT:

  • View patient medical history
  • Read patient chat messages
  • Access test results or reports
  • View clinical notes
  • Access pregnancy health details
