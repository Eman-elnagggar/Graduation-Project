Quick Reference Guide

Always Avoid

  • Ibuprofen & Aspirin (3rd trimester)
  • Retinoids (Accutane, Retin-A)
  • Certain antibiotics (Tetracycline)
  • High-dose Vitamin A
  • Alcohol-based products

Use with Caution

  • Caffeine (limit to 200mg/day)
  • Herbal supplements
  • Essential oils
  • Artificial sweeteners
  • Some hair dyes

Generally Safe

  • Prenatal vitamins
  • Paracetamol (as directed)
  • Folic acid supplements
  • Iron supplements
  • Calcium supplements
